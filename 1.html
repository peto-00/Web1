L'Afrique

I want to know more about its history, its culture and people living there.
I want to work in some parts of Africa, someday in my life. I want to travel in there.
It's a sort of groundless love.
However, it does not mean that I am not scared of malaria and yellow fever. Actually, I am a coward.

Above all, it's a good thing that there is something which consistently catches my eyes.
It's a good thing that I become having interests about a subject.
In this folder 'L'Afrique', I want to scrab manifold information about Africa. Because I want to leave some memories of my passion toward Africa, and want to share them with others who are also interested in these stuffs.
The reason why I created the name of the folder, L'Afrique is that simply, I love French.
